Understanding the Challenge

What is Autism?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental condition affecting communication, social interaction, and behavior. It is called a "spectrum" because symptoms vary widely in type and severity.

Key Characteristics

  • 🗣️Difficulty with verbal & non-verbal communication
  • 🤝Challenges in social interaction & understanding cues
  • 🔄Restricted, repetitive patterns of behavior
  • 🔊Sensory sensitivities to sound, light, or touch

Speech Challenges

  • 📉25–30% of children with ASD are minimally verbal
  • 🎙️Difficulty with voice modulation and pitch control
  • 😶Speech delay is often the first sign of ASD
  • 😰Traditional therapy can feel intimidating

The Problem

Why We Built SpeakQuest

  • 😫
    Limited Engagement — Children find clinical speech exercises boring and stressful
  • 💰
    High Cost — Regular sessions with speech pathologists are expensive and inaccessible
  • 🌍
    Accessibility Gap — Many families, especially in developing countries, lack access to therapists
  • 📅
    Inconsistent Practice — Children need daily practice, but therapy is typically weekly
  • 📊
    No Tracking — Parents and doctors lack quantitative data on speech progress
Our Solution

SpeakQuest

A free, browser-based speech therapy game that turns voice exercises into fun, interactive play — accessible to any child with a microphone.

Game Modules

Three Progressive Levels

🐦
Level 1 — Volume

Bird Flight

Control a bird's altitude with voice volume. Speak louder to fly higher and collect golden stars.

🔤
Level 2 — Articulation

Vowel Finder

Say the correct vowel sound (A, E, I, O, U) to unlock the path. Uses browser Speech Recognition.

😊
Level 3 — Expression

Emotion Match

Match displayed emotions by speaking in the right tone. Audio energy bands detect emotional intensity.

Architecture

System Architecture

Frontend (Browser)

  • ⚛️React SPA with screen-based routing
  • 🎙️Web Audio API for real-time mic analysis
  • 🗣️SpeechRecognition for vowel detection
  • 🎮Canvas API for Bird Flight game
  • 💾localStorage for offline-first data

Backend (Django)

  • 🔐JWT Authentication (register/login)
  • 📝REST API for session recording
  • 👤User profiles with child settings
  • 📊Aggregated statistics endpoint
  • 🗄️SQLite database storage
